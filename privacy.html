Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 14, 2026

Port Canyamel SL ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile applications and services (collectively, the "Services").

1. Data Collection and Storage

We are committed to user privacy. We do not collect, store, or process any personal data, email addresses, or user information on our servers. You are not required to create an account to use our Services, and we do not maintain a database of user profiles.

2. Third-Party Services

While we do not collect data directly, we utilize trusted third-party services to provide necessary infrastructure, analytics, and functionality for our applications. These third parties may collect and process certain data (such as device identifiers, usage statistics, and purchase history) in accordance with their own privacy policies.

The third-party services we use include:

  • RevenueCat: Used for managing in-app subscriptions and purchase validation.
  • Firebase: Used for analytics, crash reporting, and backend infrastructure.
  • Appsflyer: Used for mobile attribution and marketing analytics.
  • AdMob: Used for displaying in-app advertisements.
